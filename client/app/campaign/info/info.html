How it works

Entusic let’s fans book artists directly by harnessing the power of the crowd. Fans create campaigns to get their favorite artists to their city by committing to by a ticket. Once enough fans commit, the artist is confirmed and the show is booked and announced. If enough fans don’t commit when time runs out, everyone gets a refund.
